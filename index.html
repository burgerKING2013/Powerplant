📘 PowerPlantΦ Documentation

A custom VM-based language with a built-in physics engine.

System Architecture

PowerPlantΦ translates source code into bytecode executed by a stack-based Virtual Machine.

SOURCE → TOKENIZER → COMPILER → BYTECODE → VM → OUTPUT

Variables & Math

α ← 5
β ← 10
π α + β  Prints 15

Physics Engine

Built-in formulas for Force ($F=ma$), Energy ($E=\frac{1}{2}mv^2$), and Velocity ($v=u+at$).

π F 10 2  Result: 20
π E 5 3   Result: 22.5
